The discovery

My name is Martha Willow and I am an author, professor and historian specialized in the history of Nova Scotia in the 18th century. In particular, I study the blended knowledge, vocabulary and customs that came out of the interactions between the First Nation tribes and the original settlers.

A few weeks ago I found an old envelope tightly folded in the spine of an old Bible under the eaves in a barn that was being demolished near a village called Lunenburg, on the South Shore of Nova Scotia.

The envelope contained a letter and two maps, hidden there by a local man called John Creighton in March 1782 and they describe a mysterious and powerful 'artefact' along with clues to its location. I will let you read his letter, it will give you all the information you need.

You can read more about John Creighton and his capture and release in the notes I added below. Knowledge of either is not required to complete the quest.
