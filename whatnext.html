I asked a friend to recommend someone clever, based in Lunenburg and who could keep a secret and he recommended you. I am taking a risk sharing all this with you without even having met you, but I am running out of time. I hope I am not making a mistake in entrusting you with this quest.

I am leaving town for a long overdue field trip for a few week for my actual work: they will follow me thinking I will take them to the location of the artefact, and this should buy you enough time to find the artefact.



Over to you ...

How it ends

When you have found the location of the artefact, you'll need to email me the following information:

  • A distance, with units
  • A cardinal direction
  • A point of origin

For example: 67 rods, North West from the Gravel Wharf
Or: 18 rods, South of Tar Beach

I will then go and retrieve it and send it to you for safekeeping.


If you need to contact me, you can email me at the address I give you on this website (you can use it safely, they are not aware of the existence of this website).

Your help is invaluable and I am very grateful we can collaborate on this quest. Whatever the artefact is, it must not fall into the hands of those who want to misuse it and I look forward to sending it to you for safekeeping.
